📖 How to Use the Dashboard

Auto Mode – data is read live from Firebase, then analyzed using the built-in scoring engine.

Manual Mode – enter your own pH, temperature, and turbidity, then click Analyze to get predictions.

  • Theme Toggle – switch between dark and light themes.
  • Live Indicator – blinks green to confirm the system is active.
  • Fish & Spice Predictions – best species for current water conditions, with confidence scores.
  • Risk & Quality – overall risk level and water quality assessment.
  • Water Management Advice – specific actionable steps for each parameter.
  • Dataset Comparison Alerts – compares current readings against the reference dataset for the predicted fish species.
  • Rankings – lists of compatible fish and herbs/spices ordered by suitability.

Data Source: The dashboard reads from Firebase path /aquaculture/sensors. Ensure your ESP8266 is writing data there.
